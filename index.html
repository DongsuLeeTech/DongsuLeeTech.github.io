I collaborated with Prof. Ding Zhao in the Safe AI Lab, Carnegie Mellon University (CMU); with Prof. Minhae Kwon in the BMIL; and Prof. Sangsoo Kim in the Post-genome Informatics Lab at Soongsil University in Seoul, South Korea.

The research question I focus on is simple these days: Will reinforcement learning (RL) methods be applicable for practical deployment in realistic settings? I‚Äôm interested in real-world challenges, e.g, partial observability, coordination with unseen partners, and human-robot collaboration.
