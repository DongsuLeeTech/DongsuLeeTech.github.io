Dongsu Lee

Hello! I'm Dongsu. I am a first year PhD student at the University of Texas at Austin, United States, supervised by Prof. Amy Zhang (MIDI-Lab). Previously, I collaborated at Safe AI Lab (Prof. Ding Zhao and Yaru Niu) at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) in United States, BMIL (Prof. Minhae Kwon), and Post-genome Informatics Lab (Prof. Sangsoo Kim) at the Soongsil University in Seoul, South Korea.

I am interested in reinforcement learning, representation learning, and robotics. Recently, I explore the way to enhance the scalability of RL for robotics and multi-agent system.
